About Us

Started by late Mr. M. Akram, today Ghazi Cables stands outs as one of the most reliable and recognized manufacturers in its industry in Pakistan.

Started by Mr. M. Akram as a modest organization in the 1970s, Ghazi Cables has come a long way in establishing itself as a leading company in the cables and heaters industry. Since inception, Ghazi Cables has been setting standards in quality & safety with all its products. Our plant located at Sagiyaan, Lahore, is equipped with the most sophisticated manufacturing and testing equipment. Quality is the hallmark of our products, which has been sustained throughout the period.

Ghazi Cables was established on the philosophy of delivering products to the customers in time and accordingly established a vast network of dealers, the largest in its category, scattered throughout Pakistan, which still is successfully operational. To cater industrial and infrastructural projects more closely, we have also developed an efficient and genial sales team which remains available to service its valued customers.
